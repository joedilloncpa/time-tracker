13 — Guidelines

Design Principles

01
Warm, Not Clinical
Accounting tools are often cold and sterile. The cream background, serif headings, and burnt orange accent create warmth without sacrificing professionalism.
02
Orange Is Precious
Burnt orange draws the eye. Use it only for CTAs, section labels, active states, and critical highlights. Never decorate with it — only signal.
03
Three Surface Modes
Cream for the app, near-black for dense content sections, forest green for brand-forward moments (sidebar, marketing panels). Never mix arbitrarily.
04
Data Clarity First
Numbers should be instantly readable. Use DM Mono for all time values and currency. Letter-spacing -0.03em on large numerics. Tables are never decorative.
05
Serif for Humanity
DM Serif Display on headings carries the voice of a trusted advisor, not a dashboard tool. Use it for page titles, section headings, and marketing copy.
06
White Cards on Cream
Cards are white (#ffffff) on the cream background — a subtle but important distinction. Never use cream-on-cream. Borders at #ddd9d0, shadow-sm for lift.
